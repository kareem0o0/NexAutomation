Support & FAQ

HMI, PLC & IoT Questions

Common questions from factories in Egypt about HMI panel compatibility, lead times, and industrial IoT integration.

01

Are your HMI panels compatible with Siemens, Schneider, and Delta PLCs?

Yes. Our typical projects involve Siemens S7/TIA Portal, Schneider, and Delta PLCs. We use the appropriate communication drivers (Profinet/Modbus/etc.) and always validate tags and data types during commissioning. If you share your PLC model list, we can confirm compatibility before starting.

02

What lead times should we expect for HMI panels and gateways?

For common 7" and 10" HMI models we usually work from local or regional stock, with typical lead times of 1–3 weeks. Larger 15" panels, industrial PCs, and special gateways may require 4–6 weeks. We will confirm lead time and reserve hardware when we prepare your quotation.

03

Can you work on existing panels, or only supply new ones?

We can support both. Many projects start with a site assessment of existing HMIs and PLCs, then we either standardize screens, add IoT gateways, or replace legacy panels with modern ones. Our goal is to minimize downtime and reuse wiring, PLC logic, and panels where it makes sense.

04

How do you handle security for remote access and IoT?

We follow best practices such as VPN or secure tunnelling, user access levels, audit trails, and encrypted MQTT/TLS connections. We never expose PLCs directly to the internet. During design we coordinate with your IT team to align ports, firewalls, and authentication.

05

Do you provide PLC programming and training for our team?

Yes. NexAutomation can support PLC logic modifications related to HMI integration, data logging, and OEE calculations. We also run focused training sessions for maintenance and production teams on HMI use, basic diagnostics, and safe change management.
